Our Mission

Tahanan away from the Philippines, where the heart of our mission lies.


Our mission is to connect to our roots, support local artisans, and preserve traditions.


When you walk in to GES we aim to momentarily transport you to the "probinsya", country side of the Phillipines, where the pace of life is much slower. We invite you to take the time to appreciate the subtleties of natural materials made by hand.


Embracing all the nostalgic memories and warmth of a Filipino ancestral home with a millennial touch.


Also taking every opportunity to celebrate both, Amos’ (Samoan) and Darlene’s (Filipina) cultural backgrounds as we both know the importance of being connected to our roots and remembering where we came from.


Amongst the Filipino decor, you will also find mindfully curated indoor plants for your home.
